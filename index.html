OUR HISTORY

CS:GO

Fnatic’s CS:GO team is one of the most successful of all time. In our invincible period in 2013 to 2015, we picked up three Major titles and dominated every tournament with a core of Swedish superstars. Despite being in the game for a long time, Fnatic’s core CS:GO lineup is still one of the world’s most feared. With some talented youthful additions, Fnatic CS:GO is building for the next invincible era of Black and Orange domination.
