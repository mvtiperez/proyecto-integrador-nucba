Oleksandr Kostyliev

Country, City: Ukraine, Kylv

Birth: 02.10.1997 (23 years)

Role: Awper

Game Experience 2013 (8Years)

One of the best AWP in the world and one of the most unbalancing today. With a past in FlipSid3 and HellRaisers, the Ukrainian undertook the North American adventure with Team Liquid, with which he achieved the runner-up in the Major of ESL One Cologne 2016. Shortly after, he signed for Natus Vincere with whom he has achieved titles of the stature from ESL One New York 2016 or Dreamhack Winter 2017. In addition, according to HLTV.org, Sasha was recognized as the best player in the world in 2018.
